WebApp Security Challenges

Dive into the intricate world of web application security with our specially crafted challenges. Designed to test and enhance your skills, these challenges cover a broad spectrum of vulnerabilities and attack vectors that modern web applications face. From SQL injection to cross-site scripting, our scenarios are built to reflect real-world security issues, providing a comprehensive and practical learning experience. Whether you are a beginner looking to understand the basics or an experienced professional seeking to sharpen your skills, our WebApp Security Challenges offer something for everyone. Engage in hands-on problem-solving, learn new techniques, and stay ahead in the ever-evolving field of cybersecurity.

Game Rules & Regulations

1. Open to all individuals/teams registered via the Encryptia Security CTF registration form.
2. Teams of 1-4 members. Participants may join multiple teams but can only compete on one team at a time.
3. Provide a unique team name during registration.
4. Solve challenges via the official platform. Unauthorized access to challenges is prohibited.
5. Any form of cheating or hacking into other teams' systems results in disqualification.
6. Points based on challenge difficulty. Highest score wins.
7. Ties resolved by the shortest completion time.
8. Prizes for top 1st team members. Details announced before the competition.
9. Winners will be notified via email and announced on our website/social media.
10. Compete at your own risk, agreeing to all terms and conditions.
